🌿 Simple Ways to Care for the Environment

🌱 Plant trees and grow plants to improve air quality and reduce carbon footprint.

♻️ Recycle and reuse materials like plastic, glass, and paper.

🚴 Use bicycles or walk for short distances to reduce pollution.

💡 Save electricity by turning off lights and devices when not in use.

💧 Conserve water by fixing leaks and using water wisely.

🧹 Keep surroundings clean and avoid littering in public places.

📚 Spread awareness and encourage others to take care of the environment.

🛍️ Carry reusable bags when shopping instead of using single-use plastic bags.

🔋 Use renewable energy sources like solar power if possible.

🍂 Compost organic kitchen waste to reduce landfill load and create natural fertilizer.

🌍 Fun Environmental Facts

  • 🌳 One tree can absorb 22 kg of carbon dioxide per year.
  • 🧴 Plastic can take up to 1,000 years to decompose in landfills.
  • 🌎 Earth Day is celebrated on April 22 every year.
  • 🚰 Turning off your tap while brushing can save up to 8 gallons of water per day!
